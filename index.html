Subject:
URGENT: Humanitarian Request for Satellite Connectivity (GSA SIN 33411)
Email Body:
Dear Sir/Madam, I am writing to urgently request assistance regarding the ongoing nationwide telecommunications blackout in Iran that began on February 28, 2026. At present, the Iranian population is experiencing complete digital darkness. Millions of people are disconnected from the global internet and from communication with one another. Access to information during crises is critically important. Without independent communication channels, the government controls the information reaching the public. Consequently, citizens are unable to easily access reliable news or verify what is happening within the country. Furthermore, there are currently no reliable or widely used systems to warn civilians about incoming attacks or emergency situations. Internet access is urgently needed so that people can receive evacuation alerts, safety instructions, and real-time updates that could help protect them and their families. For these reasons, we urge the swift deployment of satellite internet terminals. The administrative pathway already exists: Starlink hardware (SIN 33411) and Priority Services (SIN 517410) are available through SpaceX's GSA Multiple Award Schedule (MAS). We respectfully request: 1. Immediate procurement through the GSA schedule. 2. Rapid activation of services to support civilian protection. 3. Assistance in enabling the entry and distribution of devices to those in need. Providing connectivity would help ensure that civilians can access life-saving information, maintain communication, and stay informed during this critical situation. Sincerely, [Your name, surname - city]

I am formally requesting that the (U.S. Government/SpaceX) take immediate steps to provide and facilitate the entry of satellite internet terminals (Starlink) for Iranian civilians for the following critical reasons: Preventing Massacres: Open connectivity allows the international community to monitor the situation in real-time, serving as a deterrent against human rights atrocities that thrive in isolation. The administrative pathway already exists: Starlink hardware (SIN 33411) and Priority Services (SIN 517410) are available through SpaceX's GSA Multiple Award Schedule (MAS). Civilian Safety: Access to global internet is vital for civilians to navigate active conflict zones, find medical assistance, and communicate with family members during the ongoing strikes. We respectfully request: 1. Immediate procurement through the GSA schedule. 2. Rapid activation of services to assist with civilian protection. 3. Facilitation of the entry and distribution of devices to those in need. Restoring connectivity would allow civilians to access critical information, maintain independent communication, and remain informed during this extremely difficult period. Thank you for your immediate attention to this matter. Sincerely, [Your name, surname - city]
